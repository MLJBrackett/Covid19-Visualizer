Recommended Purchases

Masks


When worn properly, a person wearing a non-medical mask or face covering can reduce the spread of his or her own infectious respiratory droplets.

Gloves


While not protecting you entirely from germs wearing gloves while using shopping carts, cleaning, or caring for other people is important to protect yourself

Hand Sanitizer


Use Hand Sanitizer with at least 60% alchohol to be effective against the virus. In addition you should regularly wash your hands with soap for at least 20 seconds

Disinfecting Wipes


Wiping surfaces with disinfecting wipes can clean the surface and remove the virus.
